What are the PaintMeter shipping hours?   TOP

We ship orders Monday - Friday between the hours of 8:30 AM - 4:00 PM.

Orders placed after 4:00 PM EST will not be processed until the following business day.

Are there restrictions for expedited shipping?   TOP

We ask that any order placed with expedited shipping be completed by 1:00 PM EST.

If you have any special needs regarding expedited shipping, please call us at 1-248-650-0500.

How long will it take before my order is shipped?   TOP

What countries does PaintMeter ship to?   TOP

We are only authorized to distribute products in the United States & Canada.

Can I use a package forwarding company?   TOP

No, we cannot ship our products to any package forwarding company.

If a shipping address to a package forwarding company is provided, we will contact you and ask for a valid business or residential address located in either the United States or Canada.

Other Shipping Restrictions   TOP

Due to our distribution restrictions, we are only able to ship to US & Canadian business/residential addresses, and must be accompanied with a US or Canadian billing address.

Warranty

PaintMeter provides a comprehensive warranty policy.

Elcometer gauges are covered by warranty for 12 months after date of delivery. Except for fair wear and tear, Elcometer warrants products against defects caused by faulty design, materials or workmanship. At the company's discretion faulty gauges or probes will be replaced or repaired, free of charge, if a fault develops within the warranty period.

Warranties through a Distributor or Dealer?

It should be noted that most claims for warranty through Distributors and Dealers are accepted up to 15 months after the date of manufacture as it is recognized that distribution of product to an End-User can take some time and gauges are sometimes not used immediately they are delivered. However, there have been occasions when claims for warranty have been made by customers beyond the warranty period and these can not be accepted or supported.

If you have any additional questions regarding warranty claims, please contact the sales department
